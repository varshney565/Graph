Lower Bound and Upper Bound on a vector ...

1 : Vector is sorted in increasing order ...

    Upper_bound : will give the element greater than the element that we are tring to find upper_bound of
    lower_bound : will give the element greater than or equal to the element that we are tring to find upper_bound of

2 : vector is sorted in decreasing order
    upper_bound : will give the element lesser than the element that we are trying to find the upper_bound of
    lower_bound : will give the element lesser than or equal to the element that we are trying to find the upper_bound of
